## Introduction

Follow the steps in the following pages to create an app with Maia Plan:

1. [Add project context](/developerportal/maia-plan/project-context/). Providing context is the foundation for Maia to build your project plan. Give Maia clear goals, user details, and requirements to generate an accurate app plan.
2. [Define the scope](/developerportal/maia-plan/define-scope/). Work with Maia to refine and detail the project scope, including the goal, success criteria, target users, and requirements.
3. [Generate the plan](/developerportal/maia-plan/generate-plan/). Maia translates your defined scope into a concrete plan, which includes a series of epics and stories.
4. [Start the development](/developerportal/maia-plan/start-development/). Organize and start working on user stories.
